about us

the best place to enjoy life.

The five-star superior CityZen Hotel boasts an extravagant and luxurious interior, the very finest cuisine and an exclusive and private atmosphere. The 60 individually designed junior suites and suites offer plenty of space and direct views of Lake St. Moritz. Guests in the Penthouse Suite have the exclusive privilege of a 360-degree view of the Alpine scenery. The sun terrace is the ideal place to sit back, relax and enjoy a cup of afternoon tea. A spa area covering 1200 sqm has numerous secluded spots where guests can unwind in peace and quiet. The Restaurant «Romanoff» serves traditional Swiss dishes, while the «Da Vittorio – St. Moritz» offers a range of Lombard specialities for which it has been awarded 1 Michelin star and 18 GaultMillau Points.

Spa & Care

Mountains envelop us with their silence: the quiet sigh of the mountain wind, the distant morning views of the rising sun, and the peaceful atmosphere of dusk and the evening hours. Covering 1,200 square metres, the CityZen Spa is spread across three floors and boasts and indoor and outdoor pool, a spacious spa area and the luxuriously appointed treatment rooms offering personal well-being. Every spa treatment is individually adapted by our experienced therapists to your personal wishes in terms of beauty, well-being and inner harmony. The combination of all-natural active substances and our high-quality treatment expertise ensures both outstanding results and lasting recovery and relaxation. Welcome to the CityZen Spa, at more than 1800 metres above sea level.
